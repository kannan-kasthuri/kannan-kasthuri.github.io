Kasthuri Kannan, PhD

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Pathology at New York University, School of Medicine. I am a computational data scientist with expertise in genomics (especially cancer) and image processing. Strong data-driven skills include machine learning and statistics. Expert in analyzing large-scale data sets and mathematical modeling. My science interest is diverse and includes cancer biology, formal logic, physical theories, computing, and mathematics. I am a part of Applied Bioinformatics Labs and Genome Technology Center at NYU. Here is my official NYU page.
